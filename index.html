SVS Street View Sequences

Welcome to the SVS dataset

SVS is a large urban dataset for place recognition, change detection and prediction from image sequences taking from different viewpoints and times. This dataset can be used to train and test both sequential and single image algorithms for place recognition and change prediction. The dataset consist of more than 1.5 million Google Street View images acquired from 10 different cities between 2007 and 2018 with high-quality ground-truth geo-locations. The large size of the dataset enables training of deep learning methods.
